Welcome to the CARE program, a Community for AI Research and Education. C.A.R.E. is an independent forum with the mission to make guidance and mentorship opportunities accessible to early-career researchers.

If you would like to get in touch with any one of our community members, please check out the volunteers on the community page. Before registering via the mentee sign-up form, please take a look at the mentors' schedules.
